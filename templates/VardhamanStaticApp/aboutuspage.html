Our Story
Meet Mrs. Kothari, an ordinary woman with an extraordinary story that began nearly two decades ago, on the 26th of January, 1996 to be precise. What an auspicious day! Hailing from a traditional Marwari family, the chances of working in a full-time job were bleak. But she was armed with a masters degree in Economics, and that’s where she drew her strength from. Her family encouraged her to start something on her own. “I picked up a proposal that came from a relative,” she reminisces, “and from thereon, I learnt the technique of turning black peppercorns into white.” With an independent loan of Rs 3Lakhs, she learnt the ropes of business quickly, and what started as a one-machine factory, today, we have nearly 13 machines for different processes.
“We spent a month in marketing our goods,” she says. Together with a lean team, they picked up the raw material, processed it, and delivered it, and before they knew it, they had acquired close to hundred buyers in 3 years.
What has been the business high? “Delivering 6,000kgs of white pepper, but one of the other greatest events is when my son joined Everest Masalas. But what keeps me humble are also the lows – a buyer once misused our produce. That was a learning for us, but we have never looked back since,” she says with determination in her eyes.
Quality is the calling card. Our production involves no malpractices, no adulteration, and with Mrs. involved in the process from start to finish, she oversees the factory with a hawk-eyed focus. We produce nearly 2,000kgs per day, and are equipped to produce nearly 30,000kgs for large-scale orders.
Vardhaman Spices’ vision is to grow its market across India, but they also have their eyes fixed on exports. Will she ever want to retire, put her feet up, and enjoy the fruits of her labour? “I’m doing it now anyway,” she quips, “but I don’t think I’ll ever want to retire. I want to keep working, and do more.”

Our Philosophy

Vardhaman Spices is a brand built on Quality, Scale, and Value For Money, and this is the fuel that has propelled us towards our success for the past 22 years.
We source our raw material directly from farmers, wholesalers, and auctioneers of Kerala, maintaining a quality that is core to our final output.
Using natural processes such as sun-drying, and without the use of any water, we dehusk black peppercorns into white to ensure that it retains its pungency levels. And finally, up to 16 different cuts – ranging from broken coarse to powdery dust – helps us achieve different products that can be used across applications.
Vardhaman Spices prides itself for producing up to <000kgs> of pepper a day; our factory is equipped to facilitate production of any weight and type of product, with quality that is on par with international demands and standards.
